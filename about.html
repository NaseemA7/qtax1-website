About Us

Hey there, we’re Qtax-1 — a family-owned business serving Philadelphia since 2009.

Back in 2009, the owner Raed Altayeb started out as just one person with a simple goal: to provide honest, affordable, and reliable tax services to the community. What began as a small effort soon grew into something bigger. Over time, Qtax-1 expanded into an entire team of dedicated staff who treat each other — and every client — like family.

From the very beginning, our promise has been the same: personal care comes first. When you visit our office, you’ll always find the owner Raed Altayeb or his son present, making sure you’re welcomed, supported, and guided every step of the way. We believe peace of mind doesn’t just come from numbers on a page — it comes from knowing you have real people you can count on.

Today, Qtax-1 has grown into more than just a tax office. We now provide tax preparation, payroll, notary services, immigration services, and financial guidance, while keeping the same family-first approach that built our foundation. Whether you’re filing a return, notarizing important documents, or managing payroll, we’re here to make the process smooth, accurate, and stress-free.

Our Mission

To deliver accurate, dependable, and affordable financial services while treating every client like part of our own family.

Our Vision

To be Philadelphia’s most trusted family-owned financial services firm, recognized for integrity, innovation, and genuine care for the community.

What started as one person’s mission has become a team effort — and together, we’re committed to helping our community grow, succeed, and thrive.
